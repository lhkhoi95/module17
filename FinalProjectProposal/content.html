Summary

My final project will be about Vietnamese cuisines. I will have at least 7 pages in this project: Home, How-Tos, three child pages that show how to make three (or more) popular Vietnamese cuisines (Pho, Spicy Noodles Beef, and Spring Roll), a page that shows different reviews about each cuisine, and an about page. For the structure, I would like to use the CSS Grid Display property as the main technique to position my contents.
